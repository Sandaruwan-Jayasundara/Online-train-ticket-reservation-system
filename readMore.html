Galle


A picturesque city in the southwest coast of Sri Lanka, Galle is a gem in this island country. Most famous for the Galle Fort, a spectacular structure built by the Dutch in the 16th century, Galle is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Located just 119 kms away from Colombo, Galle is easily accessible from major parts of the city. The geographical location, unique architectural style and a host of quirky shops & restaurants make Galle an idyllic getaway, especially during the monsoon.
